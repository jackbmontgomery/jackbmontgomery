# Jack Montgomery

**PhD Student in Computer Science at the University of Cape Town**

---

I am a doctoral student at the [Artificial Intelligence Research Unit (AIRU)](https://airu.org.za/)
at the University of Cape Town. My research is applying scientific machine learning methods for the
modelling and control of microbial hydroponic systems as part of the [Mi-Hy](https://www.mi-hy.eu/)
project. This research is funded by the
[EIC Pathfinder](https://eic.ec.europa.eu/eic-funding-opportunities/eic-pathfinder_en) scheme.

My software focus is on building tools, libraries and models for my research in JAX, Rust, C++ and
Julia.
